About Me

My name is Daniel Ferguson, and I was born in Panama. I am 22 years old and decided that Babson was the best choice for me to further my education. This is my first real time coding since I had tried to code before, self-learning python but never really felt as interested. I have thought that taking a class would force myself to learn more and this was the case and helped me spark my interest in coding. I have lived my entire life in Panama, and have spent many summers in Spain, since my mother’s side of the family is from Barcelona.

My father has always told me that to think about being an entrepreneur and always striving to learn something more. That’s why Babson College and its focus on entrepreneurship was very important for me. Since it was something that resounded very much with what my father had taught me. After graduation I plan on working in the USA for at least a year in order to gain some experience working here before returning to Panama to work with my family’s business. I feel that the things that I have learned by living and possibly working in the United States will greatly help my family’s business back home.
